What is the 7-minute sit workout?

The 7-minute sit workout is a simple and effective workout that focuses on strengthening your core muscles. It's called the sit workout because it involves sitting on a chair or bench while performing a series of exercises. This workout is perfect for beginners who are just starting their fitness journey and want to improve their strength and flexibility.

How to do the 7-minute sit workout

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do the 7-minute sit workout:

Step 1: Warm-up

Start with a 2-3 minutes warm-up by walking or jogging in place. This will help to increase your heart rate and prepare your body for the workout.

Step 2: Chair squats

Sit on a chair or bench and stand up, then sit back down. Repeat this movement for 30 seconds.

Step 3: Leg raises

Sit on a chair or bench and lift one leg straight out in front of you. Hold for 5 seconds, then lower your leg and repeat with the other leg. Do this for 30 seconds.

Step 4: Chair dips

Place your hands on the edge of the chair or bench and lower yourself down, then push back up. Repeat this movement for 30 seconds.

Step 5: Knee lifts

Sit on a chair or bench and lift your knees up towards your chest, alternating between each leg. Do this for 30 seconds.

Step 6: Plank

Place your forearms on the edge of the chair or bench and hold your body in a straight line for 30 seconds.

Step 7: Cool-down

Finish with a 2-3 minutes cool-down by stretching your muscles. This will help to prevent injury and reduce muscle soreness.

Tips for 7-minute sit workout

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your 7-minute sit workout:

  • Start slow and gradually increase the intensity of the workout.
  • Breathe deeply and maintain proper posture throughout the workout.
  • Use a sturdy chair or bench that can support your weight.
  • Listen to your body and take breaks if you need to.
  • Combine the 7-minute sit workout with other exercises for a well-rounded workout routine.

FAQ about 7-minute sit workout

Q: How many times a week should I do the 7-minute sit workout?

A: You can do the 7-minute sit workout 2-3 times a week, depending on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Is the 7-minute sit workout suitable for seniors?

A: Yes, the 7-minute sit workout is a great exercise for seniors who want to improve their strength and flexibility.

Q: Can I do the 7-minute sit workout at home?

A: Yes, you can easily do the 7-minute sit workout at home using a chair or bench.

Pros and Cons of 7-minute sit workout

Pros:

  • Quick and easy workout that can be done anywhere.
  • Improves core strength and flexibility.
  • Great for beginners and seniors.

Cons:

  • May not be challenging enough for advanced fitness enthusiasts.
  • Requires a sturdy chair or bench.
