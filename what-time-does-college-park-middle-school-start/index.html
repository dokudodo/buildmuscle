College Park Middle School Start Time

Preparing for the First Day of School

Preparing for the first day of school is crucial for a successful start to the academic year. Here are some tips to help you prepare:

  • Get enough sleep the night before to avoid being tired and grumpy on the first day.
  • Organize your school supplies, including textbooks, notebooks, and pens, in advance.
  • Plan your transportation to and from school, whether you will be using the school bus or driving yourself.
  • Familiarize yourself with the school's layout to avoid getting lost.
  • Choose your outfit for the first day of school and ensure it adheres to the school's dress code.
