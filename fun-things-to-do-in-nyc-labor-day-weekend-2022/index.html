Fun Things To Do In Nyc Labor Day Weekend 2022

Are you planning on spending Labor Day weekend in the Big Apple? New York City is a bustling metropolis with plenty of activities to keep you entertained. From concerts to food festivals, there’s something for everyone. In this article, we’ll go over some of the fun things you can do in NYC during Labor Day weekend 2022.

1. Visit the West Indian Day Parade

Celebrate Caribbean culture and heritage at the West Indian Day Parade in Brooklyn. This annual event takes place on Labor Day and features colorful costumes, lively music, and delicious food. Join the party and dance the day away!


2. Check out the US Open

If you’re a tennis fan, don’t miss the chance to catch some of the world’s best players at the US Open. This Grand Slam tournament takes place at the Billie Jean King National Tennis Center in Queens and attracts thousands of spectators every year.


3. Enjoy a Concert in the Park

Take advantage of the warm weather and attend a free concert in one of NYC’s parks. The SummerStage festival offers a variety of performances, from indie rock to classical music. Pack a picnic and enjoy the show!


4. Attend the Washington Square Outdoor Art Exhibit

If you’re an art lover, head to Greenwich Village for the Washington Square Outdoor Art Exhibit. This biannual event showcases the work of over 100 artists and is a great place to find unique pieces to add to your collection.


5. Go to the Feast of San Gennaro

Experience a slice of Italy at the Feast of San Gennaro in Little Italy. This 11-day festival features street vendors, live music, and delicious Italian cuisine. Don’t miss the procession of the statue of San Gennaro through the streets!


6. Take a Walk on the High Line

Take a scenic stroll on the High Line, a 1.45-mile elevated park on Manhattan’s west side. Enjoy views of the city and the Hudson River while surrounded by greenery and public art installations.


7. Visit the 9/11 Memorial and Museum

Pay your respects at the 9/11 Memorial and Museum, a moving tribute to the victims of the September 11th attacks. The museum features artifacts, photos, and personal stories that help visitors understand the impact of this tragic event.


8. Explore Central Park

Spend a day in the great outdoors at Central Park, a sprawling oasis in the heart of the city. Rent a bike, take a boat ride, or simply relax on the grass and people-watch.


9. Catch a Broadway Show

No trip to NYC is complete without seeing a Broadway show. From classic musicals like The Phantom of the Opera to new productions like Hamilton, there’s something for every taste.


10. Take a Cruise Around Manhattan

See the city from a different perspective on a cruise around Manhattan. Choose from a variety of options, including sightseeing tours, dinner cruises, and even a speedboat ride.


Final Thoughts

There you have it – ten fun things to do in NYC during Labor Day weekend 2022. Whether you’re a local or a visitor, these activities are sure to make your weekend unforgettable. Just remember to book your tickets and reservations in advance to avoid disappointment!
