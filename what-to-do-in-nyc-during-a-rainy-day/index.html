What To Do In Nyc During A Rainy Day

Rainy days in New York City can be a bummer, but don’t let the weather ruin your day. There are plenty of indoor activities that can keep you entertained and dry. Here are some ideas on what to do in NYC during a rainy day.

Visit a Museum

One of the best things to do in NYC during a rainy day is to visit a museum. There are many world-class museums in the city, such as the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the American Museum of Natural History, and the Museum of Modern Art. Not only will you stay dry, but you’ll also get to enjoy some of the best art, history, and science exhibits in the world.

Pro tip:

Check online for discounted tickets or free admission days. Many museums have special deals on certain days of the week or month.

Catch a Broadway Show

New York City is the home of Broadway, and there’s no better way to spend a rainy day than watching a musical or play. From classics like The Phantom of the Opera to new productions like Hamilton, there’s something for everyone on Broadway.

Pro tip:

Visit the TKTS booth in Times Square for discounted tickets to same-day shows.

Go Shopping

If you’re in the mood to shop, there’s no shortage of options in NYC. From high-end boutiques to discount stores, the city has it all. You can stay dry while browsing the stores at the iconic Macy’s or the trendy SoHo neighborhood.

Pro tip:

Bring an umbrella and wear comfortable shoes. You’ll be doing a lot of walking and may need to navigate crowded stores.

Try Indoor Activities

There are many indoor activities in NYC that can keep you entertained during a rainy day. You can go bowling at Bowlmor Times Square or take a cooking class at the Institute of Culinary Education. You can also try indoor rock climbing at Brooklyn Boulders or play arcade games at Dave & Buster’s.

Pro tip:

Check online for deals and discounts on these activities. Many places offer special rates on weekdays or during off-peak hours.

Explore a Library

The New York Public Library is one of the largest public libraries in the world and is a great place to spend a rainy day. You can browse the books, attend a lecture or workshop, or simply enjoy the architecture of the building.

Pro tip:

Bring a book or your laptop and find a cozy spot to read or work.

Visit an Aquarium

The New York Aquarium is a great place to spend a rainy day, especially if you have kids. You can see a variety of marine life, including sharks, sea lions, and penguins. You can also attend educational programs and feed the animals.

Pro tip:

Visit on a weekday to avoid crowds and long lines.

Go to a Movie Theater

There’s nothing like watching a movie on a rainy day, and NYC has some of the best movie theaters in the world. You can catch the latest blockbusters at AMC Empire 25 or enjoy independent films at the Angelika Film Center.

Pro tip:

Check online for showtimes and buy your tickets in advance to avoid sold-out shows.

Take a Spa Day

If you’re in the mood for some pampering, there are many spas in NYC that offer a variety of treatments, from massages to facials to manicures. You can relax and unwind while the rain pours outside.

Pro tip:

Book your spa appointment in advance, as many places can fill up quickly.

Enjoy a Meal Indoors

New York City is home to some of the best restaurants in the world, and there’s no better time to enjoy a meal indoors than on a rainy day. You can try out the latest food trend at a trendy restaurant or indulge in comfort food at a cozy diner.

Pro tip:

Check online for restaurant deals and discounts, and make reservations in advance to avoid waiting in line.

In Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do in NYC during a rainy day. Whether you’re in the mood for culture, entertainment, or relaxation, the city has something for everyone. So, don’t let the rain get you down and make the most of your day indoors.
