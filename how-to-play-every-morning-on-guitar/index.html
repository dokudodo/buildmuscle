How to Play Every Morning on Guitar

Every Morning is a beautiful song by Sugar Ray that is perfect for playing on guitar. To play this song, you'll need to know the following chords: G, C, D, and Em. The strumming pattern is Down, Down, Up, Up, Down, Up. Here are the chords and lyrics:

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to play Every Morning on guitar:

  1. Tune your guitar to standard tuning (EADGBE).
  2. Learn the chords G, C, D, and Em.
  3. Practice transitioning between the chords until you can do it smoothly.
  4. Learn the strumming pattern Down, Down, Up, Up, Down, Up.
  5. Practice the strumming pattern until you can do it smoothly.
