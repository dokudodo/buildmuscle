What is 3 Day a Week Mass Building Workout?

3 day a week mass building workout is a strength training program that focuses on building muscle mass and strength. As the name suggests, this workout routine involves working out three days a week, with rest days in between. This program is perfect for people who are looking to gain muscle mass and strength but don't have a lot of time to spend in the gym.

How to do 3 Day a Week Mass Building Workout

The first step to doing 3 day a week mass building workout is to create a workout plan. You should decide which exercises you want to do, how many sets and reps you will perform, and how much weight you will use. Here's an example of a workout plan:

  • Day 1: Chest, Shoulders, and Triceps
  • Bench press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Incline dumbbell press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Shoulder press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral raises - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Skull crushers - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  • Day 2: Back and Biceps
  • Deadlifts - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-ups - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Seated rows - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Barbell curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Hammer curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  • Day 3: Legs
  • Squats - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Leg press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg extensions - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Once you have your workout plan, it's time to start working out! Make sure you warm up before each workout and cool down afterward. You should also be sure to stretch after your workouts to prevent injury.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your 3 day a week mass building workout:

  • Make sure you are eating enough calories to support muscle growth. You should be eating a diet that is high in protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.
  • Get plenty of rest and recovery time. Your muscles need time to repair and grow after each workout.
  • Gradually increase the weight you are lifting. This will help you build strength and muscle mass over time.
  • Don't skip leg day! Your legs are the largest muscle group in your body, and working them out will help you build overall strength and muscle mass.

Solution to Common Problems

Here are some solutions to common problems that people encounter when doing 3 day a week mass building workout:

  • If you're not seeing results, make sure you are eating enough calories and getting enough rest.
  • If you're experiencing pain or discomfort, make sure you are using the correct form when performing exercises.
  • If you're bored with your workout routine, try switching up your exercises or adding in some new ones.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about 3 day a week mass building workout:

  • Q: How long does it take to see results?
  • A: It varies from person to person, but you should start to see results within a few weeks to a few months.

  • Q: Can women do 3 day a week mass building workout?
  • A: Yes, women can do this workout routine. It's a great way to build muscle and strength.

  • Q: Do I need to take supplements to see results?
  • A: No, you don't need to take supplements to see results. However, they can be helpful in supporting muscle growth and recovery.

Pros and Cons

Here are some pros and cons of 3 day a week mass building workout:

Pros:

  • It's a great way to build muscle mass and strength.
  • It's a time-efficient workout routine.
  • It's a great option for people who are new to strength training.

Cons:

  • It may not be enough for advanced lifters who are looking for more intense workouts.
  • It may not be enough for people who are looking to lose weight as well as gain muscle mass.
  • It may not be suitable for people who have injuries or medical conditions that prevent them from doing certain exercises.

Overall, 3 day a week mass building workout is a great option for people who are looking to gain muscle mass and strength but don't have a lot of time to spend in the gym. With the right workout plan, proper nutrition, and rest, you can achieve your fitness goals and build the body you've always wanted!
