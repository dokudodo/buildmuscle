How to

The first step to losing weight and gaining muscle is to create a workout routine. With a 5 day workout routine at Planet Fitness, you can target different muscle groups each day while also incorporating cardio to burn fat. Here’s how:

  1. Start with a warm-up of 5-10 minutes on a cardio machine such as the treadmill or elliptical.
  2. Day 1: Chest and Triceps (bench press, chest fly, tricep pushdown)
  3. Day 2: Back and Biceps (lat pulldown, seated row, bicep curls)
  4. Day 3: Shoulders and Abs (shoulder press, lateral raises, planks)
  5. Day 4: Legs (squats, lunges, leg press)
  6. Day 5: Full Body (deadlifts, push-ups, pull-ups)
  7. End each workout with a cooldown of 5-10 minutes on a cardio machine.

Step by Step

Now that you have a workout routine, it’s important to follow these steps to ensure success:

  1. Set realistic goals for yourself. Losing weight and gaining muscle takes time and effort.
  2. Track your progress. Keep a record of your workouts and measurements to see how far you’ve come.
  3. Eat a healthy and balanced diet. Fuel your body with the nutrients it needs to perform and recover.
  4. Stay consistent. Stick to your workout routine and don’t give up.
  5. Get enough rest and recovery. Allow your muscles to rest and recover between workouts.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maximize your 5 day workout routine at Planet Fitness:

  • Incorporate high-intensity interval training (HIIT) for an extra fat-burning boost.
  • Use proper form and technique to prevent injury and maximize results.
  • Don’t skip leg day! Leg workouts are important for overall strength and balance.
  • Switch up your workout routine every 4-6 weeks to prevent boredom and plateauing.

Solution

By following a 5 day workout routine at Planet Fitness, you can lose weight and gain muscle in a safe and effective way. With a combination of strength training and cardio, you can target different muscle groups while also burning fat. Remember to set realistic goals, track your progress, eat a healthy diet, stay consistent, and get enough rest and recovery.

FAQ

What should I eat before and after my workout?

Before your workout, eat a small snack that is high in carbohydrates and protein such as a banana with peanut butter or a protein bar. After your workout, eat a meal that is high in protein and carbohydrates to help with muscle recovery and growth.

How often should I do cardio?

It is recommended to do cardio at least 3-4 times per week for 20-30 minutes each session.

Can I do this workout routine at home?

While some exercises may require equipment, many can be modified to be done at home with minimal equipment. Consult with a personal trainer or fitness professional to determine the best options for you.
