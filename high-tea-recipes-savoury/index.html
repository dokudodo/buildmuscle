Recipe 1: Cucumber Sandwiches

Cucumber sandwiches are a classic high tea recipe that's easy to make. Here's how:

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of white bread
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 4 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tbsp. chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix the cream cheese, dill, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Spread the cream cheese mixture onto each slice of bread.
  3. Place the cucumber slices on top of the cream cheese on 4 of the slices.
  4. Top with the remaining bread slices.
  5. Cut the sandwiches into quarters and serve.

Tip: You can also add some smoked salmon or prosciutto to the sandwiches for extra flavour.

Recipe 2: Scones with Bacon and Cheese

If you're looking for a savoury twist on the classic scone, try this recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 4 tbsp. cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 3/4 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Using a pastry blender or your fingers, cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the cheese and bacon.
  5. Add the milk and stir until just combined.
  6. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until it comes together.
  7. Roll out the dough to about 1/2 inch thickness and cut out circles using a biscuit cutter.
  8. Place the circles on the prepared baking sheet and brush the tops with some milk.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

Recipe 3: Mini Quiches

Mini quiches are perfect for high tea because they're easy to serve and can be made ahead of time. Here's how:

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked ham
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a mini muffin tin.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface and cut out circles using a biscuit cutter.
  3. Place the circles into the muffin tin, pressing down gently to fit.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, cheese, ham, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pastry shells, filling each about 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the quiches are set and golden brown.
  7. Let cool in the tin for a few minutes before removing and serving.

Recipe 4: Mushroom Tartlets

These mushroom tartlets are a great vegetarian option for your high tea party:

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 8 oz. mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a mini muffin tin.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface and cut out circles using a biscuit cutter.
  3. Place the circles into the muffin tin, pressing down gently to fit.
  4. In a pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the onion and cook until softened.
  6. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their liquid and are tender.
  7. Stir in the parmesan cheese, cream, salt, and pepper.
  8. Spoon the mushroom mixture into the pastry shells, filling each about 3/4 full.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the tartlets are set and golden brown.
  10. Let cool in the tin for a few minutes before removing and serving.

Recipe 5: Spinach and Feta Rolls

These spinach and feta rolls are a delicious and healthy option for your high tea party:

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface and cut into 4 rectangles.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the spinach, feta cheese, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Divide the spinach mixture among the 4 rectangles, spreading it out in a line down the middle of each.
  5. Brush the edges of the pastry with the beaten egg.
  6. Roll up the pastry around the filling and press the edges to seal.
  7. Place the rolls seam-side down on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Brush the tops with the remaining egg.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the rolls are golden brown and crispy.
  10. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it, five delicious high tea recipes savoury that you can try for your next tea party. Whether you're a fan of sandwiches, scones, quiches, tartlets, or rolls, there's something for everyone. Don't forget to add some tea and sweets to the mix, and you'll have a memorable afternoon with your guests.
