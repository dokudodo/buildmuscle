7 Best Exercises To Build Muscle

Written by Bowie Jun 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to build muscle and get that toned physique you’ve always wanted? Look no further than these 7 exercises that are guaranteed to help you achieve your muscle-building goals. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned gym-goer, these exercises can help you get the results you want.

Table of Contents

  • Deadlifts
  • Bench Press
  • Pull-Ups
  • Dips

  • Barbell Rows

1. Squats

Squats are one of the best exercises for building muscle in your legs, glutes, and core. This compound movement engages multiple muscle groups at once, making it an efficient exercise for building strength and muscle. To perform a squat, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Bend your knees and lower your body as if you were sitting in a chair, keeping your back straight and your chest up. Return to standing by pushing through your heels.

2. Deadlifts

Deadlifts are another compound movement that engages multiple muscle groups, including your back, glutes, and hamstrings. To perform a deadlift, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Grip the barbell with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Keeping your back straight and your chest up, lift the barbell off the ground by pushing through your heels. Lower the barbell back to the ground with control.

3. Bench Press

The bench press is a classic exercise for building muscle in your chest, shoulders, and triceps. To perform a bench press, lie on a flat bench with your feet flat on the ground. Grip the barbell with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower the barbell to your chest, keeping your elbows close to your body. Push the barbell back up to the starting position.

4. Pull-Ups

Pull-ups are a great exercise for building muscle in your back, shoulders, and arms. To perform a pull-up, grip a pull-up bar with your palms facing away from you and your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Hang from the bar with your arms fully extended. Pull your body up towards the bar by engaging your back muscles. Lower your body back down with control.

5. Dips

Dips are an effective exercise for building muscle in your chest, shoulders, and triceps. To perform a dip, grip parallel bars with your palms facing down and your arms fully extended. Lower your body by bending your elbows, keeping your chest up and your shoulders back. Push your body back up to the starting position.

6. Overhead Press

The overhead press is a great exercise for building muscle in your shoulders, triceps, and upper back. To perform an overhead press, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and grip the barbell with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lift the barbell to shoulder height, keeping your elbows close to your body. Press the barbell overhead, fully extending your arms. Lower the barbell back to shoulder height with control.

7. Barbell Rows

Barbell rows are an excellent exercise for building muscle in your back and arms. To perform a barbell row, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and grip the barbell with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Bend your knees slightly and hinge forward at the hips, keeping your back straight. Lift the barbell towards your chest by engaging your back muscles. Lower the barbell back down with control.

Conclusion

These 7 exercises are some of the best for building muscle and strength. Incorporate them into your workout routine and you’ll be on your way to achieving your muscle-building goals. Remember to start with lighter weights and focus on proper form to avoid injury. Happy lifting!
