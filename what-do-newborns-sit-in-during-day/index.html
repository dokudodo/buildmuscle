Welcome to our guide on what do newborns sit in during the day. As a new parent, you may feel overwhelmed by the number of baby gear options available on the market. One of the essential items that you will need for your newborn is a safe and comfortable place for them to sit in while you go about your day. In this article, we will explore the different options available and provide you with some useful tips on how to choose the best one for your baby.

Option 1: Bouncers and Rockers

Bouncers and rockers are a popular choice for newborns. They are designed to keep your baby entertained and soothed while you go about your day. These seats usually have a vibration or rocking function that can help calm your baby down and put them to sleep. They also have adjustable positions, so you can set them up to be reclined or upright.

Some bouncers and rockers also come with toys and mobiles attached, which can keep your baby entertained and engaged. However, it is important to note that you should never leave your baby unattended in a bouncer or rocker.

Option 2: Infant Seats

Infant seats are another option for newborns. These seats are designed to be more upright than bouncers and rockers, which can help your baby develop their neck and back muscles. They also come with a tray or toy bar, which can keep your baby entertained while you go about your day.

Infant seats are also a great option if you want to feed your baby in a seated position. Some infant seats even come with a reclining function, which can be useful if your baby falls asleep while sitting in the seat.

Option 3: Floor Seats

Floor seats are another option for newborns. These seats are designed to be placed on the floor and come with a soft, supportive seat that can help your baby sit up. They also come with a tray or toy bar, which can keep your baby entertained while you go about your day.

One of the benefits of floor seats is that they are portable and can be easily moved from room to room. They are also a great option if you want to encourage your baby to sit up and develop their core muscles.

Tips for Choosing a Newborn Seat

When choosing a newborn seat, there are several factors to consider:

  • Age and weight limit: Make sure the seat you choose is suitable for your baby's age and weight.
  • Safety features: Look for seats that have a secure harness system and non-slip base.
  • Comfort: Make sure the seat has a soft, supportive seat and is comfortable for your baby to sit in.
  • Portability: Consider whether you need a seat that is portable and easy to move from room to room.
  • Price: Newborn seats can range in price, so consider your budget when choosing one.

FAQs

What is the best newborn seat?

The best newborn seat depends on your individual needs and preferences. Bouncers, rockers, infant seats, and floor seats are all good options to consider.

Can newborns sit up?

Newborns are not able to sit up on their own, but they can be supported in a seated position in a newborn seat.

When can a newborn sit in a regular seat?

Newborns should not sit in a regular seat until they have developed the neck and back muscles to support themselves. This typically happens around 6 months of age.
