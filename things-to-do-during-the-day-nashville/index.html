Things To Do During The Day In Nashville A Guide For Relaxing Adventures

If you're looking for a city with a mix of music, history, and culture, Nashville, Tennessee, is the place to be. Nashville is a vibrant city that offers a variety of activities for visitors and locals alike. From live music to museums, and outdoor adventures, there's something for everyone. In this article, we'll explore the top things to do in Nashville during the day, so pack your bags and let's get started.

Visit the Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum

The Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum is one of the top attractions in Nashville. The museum highlights the history of country music and its famous icons. Visitors can explore the exhibits, watch live performances, and learn about the evolution of country music. The museum is open daily from 9 am to 5 pm, and tickets can be purchased online or at the door.

Explore the Cheekwood Estate and Gardens

The Cheekwood Estate and Gardens is a 55-acre botanical garden and art museum. Visitors can explore the mansion, which features art exhibits, and stroll through the beautiful gardens. Cheekwood also offers various events throughout the year, such as holiday light displays and outdoor concerts. The estate is open daily from 9 am to 5 pm, and tickets can be purchased online or at the gate.

Take a Stroll at the Bicentennial Capitol Mall State Park

The Bicentennial Capitol Mall State Park is located in the heart of Nashville and is a great place for a relaxing stroll. The park features various monuments and exhibits that showcase Tennessee's history. Visitors can also enjoy the park's water features, walking trails, and beautiful views of the state's Capitol building. The park is open daily from dawn to dusk, and admission is free.

Enjoy the View from the John Seigenthaler Pedestrian Bridge

The John Seigenthaler Pedestrian Bridge is a great spot for a scenic view of downtown Nashville. The bridge spans across the Cumberland River and offers a panoramic view of the city's skyline. Visitors can walk or bike across the bridge and take in the beautiful scenery. The bridge is open 24 hours a day, and admission is free.

Visit the Adventure Science Center

The Adventure Science Center is a hands-on science museum that offers interactive exhibits and educational programs. Visitors can explore the museum's various exhibits, including space exploration, robotics, and physics. The center also offers a planetarium and live science demonstrations. The center is open daily from 10 am to 5 pm, and tickets can be purchased online or at the door.

Experience the Nashville Zoo at Grassmere

The Nashville Zoo at Grassmere is a 200-acre zoo that features various animals from around the world. Visitors can observe the animals in their natural habitats and learn about their behaviors and habitats. The zoo also offers various events throughout the year, such as animal encounters and seasonal exhibits. The zoo is open daily from 9 am to 6 pm, and tickets can be purchased online or at the gate.

Take a Tour of the Ryman Auditorium

The Ryman Auditorium is a historic venue that has hosted some of the biggest names in music. Visitors can take a backstage tour of the auditorium and learn about its history and significance in music. The tour also includes a visit to the museum, which features exhibits and memorabilia from the Ryman's past. The auditorium is open daily from 9 am to 4 pm, and tickets can be purchased online or at the door.

Shop and Dine at The Gulch

The Gulch is a trendy neighborhood in Nashville that offers various shopping and dining options. Visitors can shop at boutiques and specialty stores or dine at one of the many restaurants and cafes. The Gulch is also home to various art galleries and entertainment venues. Visitors can explore the neighborhood on foot or by using the free trolley service. The Gulch is open daily, and hours vary by establishment.

Walk or Bike the Shelby Bottoms Greenway

The Shelby Bottoms Greenway is a 960-acre park that offers various walking and biking trails. Visitors can explore the park's natural habitats and observe the local wildlife. The park also features various picnic areas and a dog park. The park is open daily from dawn to dusk, and admission is free.

Visit the Parthenon at Centennial Park

The Parthenon at Centennial Park is a full-scale replica of the ancient Greek structure. The building houses an art museum that features various exhibits and galleries. Visitors can explore the museum and learn about the history of ancient Greece. The park is also home to various walking trails and picnic areas. The Parthenon is open daily from 9 am to 4:30 pm, and tickets can be purchased online or at the door.

The Bottom Line

Nashville is a city that offers something for everyone. Whether you're interested in music, history, or outdoor adventures, Nashville has it all. From the Country Music Hall of Fame to the Shelby Bottoms Greenway, there's no shortage of things to do during the day. So, pack your bags and head to Nashville for a relaxing and adventurous getaway.
