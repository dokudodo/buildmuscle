Best After Dinner Snacks For Weight Loss

Are you struggling to keep the pounds off, but still want to enjoy a tasty snack after dinner? The good news is that there are plenty of options for healthy snacks that can help you stick to your weight loss goals. In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the best after dinner snacks for weight loss and how you can incorporate them into your diet.

Fruits

Fruits are a great option for an after dinner snack as they are low in calories and high in fiber. Some of the best fruits for weight loss include:

  • Apples
  • Berries
  • Oranges
  • Grapefruit
  • Kiwi

These fruits are also high in antioxidants, which can help to reduce inflammation in the body and may even help to prevent chronic diseases.

How to:

Try slicing up some of your favorite fruits and mixing them together for a tasty and satisfying after dinner snack. You can also add some Greek yogurt or a tablespoon of natural peanut butter for some extra protein.

Tips:

When choosing fruits, opt for fresh or frozen options rather than canned or dried fruits which may have added sugars.

Pros:

  • Low in calories
  • High in fiber
  • High in antioxidants

Cons:

  • May not be as filling as other snacks
  • Some fruits may be high in natural sugars

Yogurt

Yogurt is another great option for a healthy after dinner snack. It is a good source of protein and calcium, which can help to keep you feeling full and satisfied.

How to:

Try mixing some Greek yogurt with some fresh berries or a tablespoon of honey for a sweet and satisfying snack.

Tips:

When choosing yogurt, opt for plain or Greek yogurt rather than flavored varieties which may have added sugars.

Pros:

  • Good source of protein and calcium
  • May improve gut health

Cons:

  • Some varieties may be high in added sugars
  • May not be as filling as other snacks

Nuts

Nuts are a great option for a healthy after dinner snack as they are high in protein and healthy fats, which can help to keep you feeling full and satisfied. Some of the best nuts for weight loss include:

  • Almonds
  • Walnuts
  • Pistachios
  • Cashews

How to:

Try snacking on a handful of nuts or mixing them with some dried fruit for a tasty and nutritious snack.

Tips:

When choosing nuts, opt for raw or roasted varieties rather than salted or flavored nuts which may have added sugars or sodium.

Pros:

  • High in protein and healthy fats
  • May help to reduce inflammation in the body

Cons:

  • May be high in calories
  • May trigger allergies in some individuals

Veggies and Hummus

Veggies and hummus are a great option for a healthy after dinner snack as they are low in calories and high in fiber. The hummus also provides some protein, which can help to keep you feeling full and satisfied.

How to:

Try slicing up some carrots, cucumbers, and bell peppers and dipping them in some hummus for a tasty and nutritious snack.

Tips:

When choosing hummus, opt for varieties that are lower in sodium and made with natural ingredients.

Pros:

  • Low in calories
  • High in fiber
  • Provides some protein

Cons:

  • May not be as filling as other snacks
  • Hummus may be high in sodium

Popcorn

Popcorn can be a great option for a healthy after dinner snack as it is low in calories and high in fiber. However, it is important to choose the right type of popcorn and avoid adding too much butter or salt.

How to:

Try air-popped popcorn with a sprinkle of sea salt or some nutritional yeast for a tasty and satisfying snack.

Tips:

Avoid microwave popcorn which may be high in unhealthy fats and sodium.

Pros:

  • Low in calories
  • High in fiber
  • May have some antioxidant properties

Cons:

  • May be high in sodium or unhealthy fats if not prepared correctly
  • May not be as filling as other snacks

Smoothie

Smoothies can be a great option for a healthy after dinner snack as they are high in nutrients and can be customized to your taste preferences. However, it is important to choose the right ingredients and avoid adding too much sugar or unhealthy fats.

How to:

Try blending together some frozen fruit, Greek yogurt, and a handful of spinach or kale for a healthy and nutritious smoothie.

Tips:

Avoid using fruit juice or sweetened yogurt which may be high in added sugars.

Pros:

  • High in nutrients
  • Can be customized to your taste preferences

Cons:

  • May be high in sugar or unhealthy fats if not prepared correctly
  • May not be as filling as other snacks

Dark Chocolate

Dark chocolate can be a great option for a healthy after dinner snack as it is high in antioxidants and may even have some heart-healthy benefits. However, it is important to choose the right type of chocolate and avoid overindulging.

How to:

Try snacking on a small piece of dark chocolate or adding some cocoa powder to your Greek yogurt for a sweet and satisfying snack.

Tips:

Opt for dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa content and avoid varieties that are high in added sugars.

Pros:

  • High in antioxidants
  • May have some heart-healthy benefits

Cons:

  • May be high in calories
  • May trigger allergies in some individuals

Cottage Cheese

Cottage cheese is a great option for a healthy after dinner snack as it is high in protein and low in calories. It can also be customized to your taste preferences with some added fruit or nuts.

How to:

Try mixing some cottage cheese with some fresh fruit or a handful of nuts for a tasty and nutritious snack.

Tips:

Opt for low-fat or non-fat varieties of cottage cheese to reduce calories and fat content.
