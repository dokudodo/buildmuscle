What Is The Best 2 Day Workout Split

Written by Alvine Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a workout routine that will help you get in shape without taking up too much of your time? If so, you might be wondering what the best 2 day workout split is. In this article, we’ll explore the benefits and drawbacks of different 2 day workout splits, and help you choose the one that’s right for you.

Description

A 2 day workout split is a type of exercise routine that involves working out two times a week. This type of workout routine is ideal for people who are short on time, but still want to stay in shape. With a 2 day workout split, you can work out your entire body over the course of two days, and then rest for the rest of the week.

How to

If you’re interested in starting a 2 day workout split, there are a few different approaches you can take. Here are three examples:

Upper Body/Lower Body Split

With this workout split, you’ll focus on your upper body muscles (e.g. chest, back, shoulders, and arms) on day one, and your lower body muscles (e.g. legs and glutes) on day two. This approach is great for building strength and endurance in your entire body.

Push/Pull Split

The push/pull split is another popular 2 day workout split. On day one, you’ll focus on exercises that involve pushing movements (e.g. bench press, overhead press, and push-ups), and on day two, you’ll focus on exercises that involve pulling movements (e.g. pull-ups, rows, and deadlifts). This workout split is great for improving your overall strength and balance.

Full Body Split

A full body split involves working out your entire body on both days. This approach is great for beginners, as it allows you to get a full-body workout without overwhelming your muscles. With this workout split, you’ll do a variety of exercises that target different muscle groups.

Step by Step

Regardless of which 2 day workout split you choose, here are some general steps you can follow:

  1. Warm up for 5-10 minutes with some light cardio (e.g. jogging or jumping jacks).
  2. Do 3-5 sets of each exercise, with 8-12 reps per set.
  3. Rest for 1-2 minutes between sets.
  4. Cool down for 5-10 minutes with some stretching exercises.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your 2 day workout split:

  • Make sure you’re using proper form for each exercise.
  • Start with lighter weights and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water before, during, and after your workout.
  • Get plenty of rest and allow your muscles to recover between workouts.

Solution

A 2 day workout split is a great solution for people who want to stay in shape but don’t have a lot of time to spend at the gym. By working out just two times a week, you can improve your overall fitness and health without disrupting your busy schedule.

FAQ

What are the benefits of a 2 day workout split?

A 2 day workout split allows you to work out your entire body over the course of two days, and then rest for the rest of the week. This approach is great for building strength, improving endurance, and maintaining overall fitness.

Is a 2 day workout split enough?

It depends on your fitness goals. If you’re looking to build a lot of muscle or improve your athletic performance, a 2 day workout split might not be enough. However, if you’re looking to stay in shape and maintain your current level of fitness, a 2 day workout split can be a great option.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Great for people who are short on time
  • Allows you to work out your entire body over the course of two days
  • Can be customized to meet your fitness goals

Cons

  • May not be enough if you’re looking to build a lot of muscle or improve your athletic performance
  • Requires discipline and consistency to see results
  • May not be suitable for people with certain medical conditions or injuries

In conclusion, a 2 day workout split can be a great way to stay in shape without disrupting your busy schedule. Whether you choose an upper body/lower body split, a push/pull split, or a full body split, make sure you’re using proper form, gradually increasing the weight, and allowing your muscles to recover between workouts. With discipline and consistency, you can achieve your fitness goals and improve your overall health and well-being.
