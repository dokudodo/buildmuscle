Are you struggling to find the motivation to study? Do you need a little extra push to get you through those long study sessions? Look no further than these motivational quotes in Hindi. These quotes will inspire and motivate you to keep working hard towards your goals. Here are some of the best motivational quotes for study in Hindi:

  3. "जब तुम अपने स्वप्नों पे काम करते हो तो सपनों की संभावनाएं होती हैं।"

  5. "जब तुम ना कर सके तो उससे ज्यादा कुछ मांगो, क्योंकि ये दुनिया तुम्हारी मांगो से बड़ी होती है।"

"कोशिश करने वालों की कभी हार नहीं होती।"

Translation: "Those who make an effort never lose."

This quote reminds us that even if we fail, we should never give up. If we continue to work hard and make an effort, we will eventually succeed. So don't be afraid to try, even if you think you might fail.


"आज की कल की जुड़ी हुई दो ही शब्द हैं - पढ़ाई और कामयाबी।"

Translation: "The two words that are connected to today and tomorrow are studying and success."

This quote emphasizes the importance of studying in order to achieve success. By studying today, we can build a better future for ourselves. So make studying a priority, and success will follow.


"जब तुम अपने स्वप्नों पे काम करते हो तो सपनों की संभावनाएं होती हैं।"

Translation: "When you work towards your dreams, the possibilities become endless."

This quote is a reminder that when we work towards our dreams, anything is possible. Don't be afraid to dream big and work hard towards those dreams. The possibilities are endless.


"कुछ नहीं होगा तो नहीं होगा, पर कोशिश नहीं करोगे तो कुछ भी नहीं होगा।"

Translation: "If it doesn't happen, it doesn't happen, but if you don't try, nothing will happen."

This quote reminds us that we should never be afraid to try. If we don't try, we will never know what could have been. So take a chance and make an effort towards your goals.


"जब तुम ना कर सके तो उससे ज्यादा कुछ मांगो, क्योंकि ये दुनिया तुम्हारी मांगो से बड़ी होती है।"

Translation: "When you can't do something, ask for more, because this world is bigger than your limitations."

This quote encourages us to push beyond our limits and ask for more. Don't be afraid to ask for help or to ask for more resources to help you achieve your goals.


Conclusion

These motivational quotes in Hindi are a great way to stay motivated and inspired while studying. Remember to never give up, work towards your dreams, and always be willing to try new things. By keeping these quotes in mind, you can achieve anything you set your mind to.
