Valentine's Day is a special day to show your love and affection to your significant other. Boston has a lot of romantic things to do, and it is the perfect city to spend Valentine's Day 2022. In this article, we will give you some ideas on how to spend this day in Boston. From romantic restaurants to scenic walks, we have got you covered. Let's dive into it!

1. Dine at a Romantic Restaurant

Boston has a plethora of romantic restaurants that are perfect for a Valentine's Day dinner. Some of the best restaurants include L'Espalier, No. 9 Park, and Mistral. These restaurants offer a romantic ambiance, excellent food, and a great wine selection. Make sure to make a reservation in advance as they tend to book up quickly.

2. Take a Romantic Stroll in the Public Garden

The Boston Public Garden is a beautiful place to take a romantic walk. The garden has a lagoon, fountains, and beautiful flowers that bloom all year round. You can also take a ride on the famous swan boats that operate during the warmer months. A stroll in the Public Garden is a perfect way to spend Valentine's Day in Boston.

3. Visit the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum

The Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum is a beautiful museum that is perfect for art lovers. The museum has a collection of art from different time periods, including ancient Rome, Renaissance, and contemporary art. The museum also has a beautiful courtyard that is perfect for a romantic walk.

4. Ice Skating at the Frog Pond

The Frog Pond is a popular ice skating rink in Boston. It is located in the Boston Common and is perfect for a romantic ice skating date. The rink is open during the winter months, and it is a great way to enjoy the winter weather in Boston.

5. Visit the New England Aquarium

The New England Aquarium is a great place to visit on Valentine's Day. The aquarium has a variety of marine animals, including penguins, sea turtles, and sharks. You can also take a whale watching tour from the aquarium during the warmer months.

6. Take a Sunset Cruise

A sunset cruise is a romantic way to spend Valentine's Day in Boston. You can take a cruise on the Charles River and enjoy the beautiful views of the city. Many companies offer different types of cruises, including dinner cruises and wine tasting cruises.

7. Visit the Museum of Fine Arts

The Museum of Fine Arts is a great place to visit on Valentine's Day. The museum has a vast collection of art, including paintings, sculptures, and photographs. You can also take a guided tour of the museum to learn more about the art.

8. Take a Chocolate Tour

Boston has many chocolate shops that offer tours. A chocolate tour is a great way to learn about the history of chocolate and taste some delicious treats. Some of the best chocolate shops in Boston include Taza Chocolate, Beacon Hill Chocolates, and L.A. Burdick Chocolate.

9. Visit the Boston Symphony Orchestra

The Boston Symphony Orchestra is a great place to spend Valentine's Day. The orchestra performs at the Symphony Hall, and you can enjoy a beautiful performance of classical music. Make sure to book your tickets in advance as they tend to sell out quickly.

10. Take a Helicopter Tour

A helicopter tour is a unique way to see Boston from above. You can take a helicopter tour of the city and enjoy the beautiful views of the skyline. Many companies offer different types of tours, including sunset tours and private tours.

Conclusion

Valentine's Day 2022 in Boston is going to be a special day. We hope our list of things to do in Boston helps you plan the perfect Valentine's Day with your significant other. Whether you want to dine at a romantic restaurant or take a helicopter tour, Boston has something for everyone. Happy Valentine's Day!
