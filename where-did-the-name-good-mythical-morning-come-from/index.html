History of Good Mythical Morning

Good Mythical Morning is a YouTube show that was created by two childhood friends, Rhett McLaughlin and Link Neal. They later decided to start their own YouTube channel, which eventually led to the creation of Good Mythical Morning.

The name Good Mythical Morning was not the duo's first choice for the show's title. In fact, they had several other names in mind, including "The Morning Show with Rhett and Link" and "Rhett and Link's Excellent Morning Show."

However, they eventually settled on the name Good Mythical Morning, which they thought was more unique and memorable. The "Mythical" part of the name comes from the duo's love for mythology and their desire to incorporate it into the show's content. The "Morning" part of the name was added to signify that the show was meant to be watched in the morning.

The Rise to Fame of Good Mythical Morning

Good Mythical Morning's popularity skyrocketed due to its unique blend of humor, entertainment, and educational content. The show's format is simple, with Rhett and Link discussing various topics, playing games, and conducting experiments.
