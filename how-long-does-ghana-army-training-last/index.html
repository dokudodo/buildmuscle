Introduction

The Ghana Army is one of the leading military forces in Africa. It is responsible for the defence and protection of the country's borders, as well as the maintenance of internal security. If you are interested in joining the Ghana Army, one of the questions you might be asking is, "How long does Ghana Army training last?" In this guide, we will provide you with a comprehensive answer to this question.

How Long Does Ghana Army Training Last?

The Ghana Army training lasts for six months. This period includes both basic military training and specialized training for specific roles. During this intensive training period, recruits are prepared physically, mentally, and emotionally for the rigours of military life. The training is conducted at the Army Recruit Training School (ARTS) in Shai Hills, Ghana.

Unlike other military forces, the Ghana Army does not have a mandatory national service programme. Instead, all recruits must undergo the six-month training programme at ARTS before they can be considered for deployment.

What Happens During Ghana Army Training?

The Ghana Army training programme is designed to equip recruits with the necessary skills and knowledge to become effective soldiers. The training is divided into two phases: Basic Military Training (BMT) and Specialized Training.

The Basic Military Training is the first phase of the training programme. It is designed to instil discipline, physical fitness, and teamwork in the recruits. During this phase, recruits are subjected to a rigorous physical training regime that includes running, marching, push-ups, sit-ups, and obstacle courses. They also undergo weapons training, map reading, and first aid training. The aim of this phase is to develop the recruits' physical and mental resilience, as well as their ability to work in a team.

The second phase of the training programme is the Specialized Training. This phase is designed to provide recruits with specialized skills and knowledge for specific roles in the Ghana Army. Recruits are trained in areas such as infantry, artillery, armour, signals, and logistics. The aim of this phase is to develop the recruits' technical skills and knowledge, as well as their ability to operate effectively in their chosen field.

How to Join the Ghana Army

If you are interested in joining the Ghana Army, there are certain requirements that you must meet. These include:

  • Being a citizen of Ghana
  • Being between the ages of 18 and 26
  • Having a minimum of six credits (including English and Mathematics) at the WASSCE level
  • Being physically and mentally fit
  • Passing the Ghana Armed Forces Recruitment Test

If you meet these requirements, you can apply to join the Ghana Army by visiting the Ghana Armed Forces recruitment portal. The portal is open to both men and women, and the recruitment process is highly competitive. Only the most qualified candidates are selected for training.
