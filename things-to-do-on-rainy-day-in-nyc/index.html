Things To Do On Rainy Day In Nyc

When it’s raining outside, it can be tempting to stay indoors and binge-watch your favorite show. But don’t let the rain ruin your plans! There are plenty of fun things to do on a rainy day in NYC that will keep you entertained and dry. In this article, we will provide you with a list of activities you can do in the city that never sleeps, even on a rainy day.

Visit Museums

New York City is home to some of the best museums in the world, and they are the perfect place to spend a rainy day. You can choose from a variety of museums, including the American Museum of Natural History, the Metropolitan Museum of Art, or the Museum of Modern Art. You can spend hours exploring the exhibits and learning new things. Plus, many museums offer free admission on certain days, so you can save some money while staying dry.

Catch a Movie

Going to the movies is always a great option on a rainy day. There are plenty of movie theaters throughout the city, including chains like AMC and Regal, as well as independent theaters like the IFC Center and the Angelika Film Center. You can catch up on the latest blockbusters or check out a classic film on the big screen.

Go Shopping

If you’re in the mood to do some shopping, there are plenty of indoor options in New York City. You can head to one of the many malls in the city, such as the Westfield World Trade Center or the Manhattan Mall. Or, you can explore some of the unique boutiques and shops in neighborhoods like SoHo or the Lower East Side.

Relax in Spas

When it’s raining outside, there’s nothing better than a relaxing day at the spa. New York City is home to some of the best spas in the world, offering a variety of services from massages to facials. You can spend the day pampering yourself and escaping the rain.

Visit Arcades

If you’re looking for something fun to do with friends or family, consider visiting an arcade. There are plenty of arcades throughout the city, such as Dave & Buster’s or Barcade. You can play classic games like Pac-Man or try your hand at some of the newer games.

Head to Libraries

For a quieter activity on a rainy day, head to one of the many libraries in New York City. You can curl up with a good book, use the free Wi-Fi, or attend a literary event. Some of the best libraries to visit include the New York Public Library, the Brooklyn Public Library, and the Queens Library.

Hang out in Cafes

If you’re looking for a cozy spot to spend a rainy day, head to one of the many cafes in the city. You can grab a cup of coffee or tea and settle in with a good book or some work. Some of the best cafes to check out include The Uncommons, Bluestone Lane, and Ground Support Cafe.

Explore Aquariums

Another great indoor activity on a rainy day is visiting an aquarium. New York City is home to the New York Aquarium in Coney Island, which features exhibits on marine life from around the world. You can see sharks, sea turtles, and other aquatic creatures up close and learn more about their habitats and behaviors.

Attend Concerts

If you’re a music lover, attending a concert is a great way to spend a rainy day. There are plenty of venues throughout the city that offer concerts, from the legendary Madison Square Garden to smaller venues like the Bowery Ballroom. You can check out some of your favorite artists or discover new ones.

Watch Broadway Shows

Finally, no trip to New York City is complete without seeing a Broadway show. There are plenty of theaters throughout the city, offering everything from classic shows like The Phantom of the Opera to newer productions like Hamilton. You can see some of the best performances in the world and escape the rain for a few hours.

Tips for Enjoying a Rainy Day in NYC

  • Bring an umbrella or raincoat to stay dry when walking between activities.
  • Check the weather forecast before heading out to plan your day accordingly.
  • Consider using public transportation or ride-sharing services to avoid getting wet.
  • Bring a change of clothes or shoes in case you get wet.

Pros and Cons of Rainy Days in NYC

While rainy days in NYC can be a bummer, there are some benefits and drawbacks to consider.

Pros:

  • Indoor activities like museums and theaters are less crowded on rainy days.
  • You can avoid the heat and humidity that often come with summer in the city.
  • There’s something cozy and comforting about curling up with a book or watching a movie while it’s raining outside.

Cons:

  • You may have to deal with wet and slippery sidewalks and streets.
  • Outdoor activities like walking tours or picnics are not possible on rainy days.
  • You may have to wait longer for public transportation or ride-sharing services due to increased demand.

FAQs

Q: What should I wear on a rainy day in NYC?

A: It’s a good idea to wear waterproof shoes or boots, a raincoat or jacket, and an umbrella.

Q: Are museums open on rainy days?

A: Yes, most museums are open year-round, including on rainy days.

Q: Can I still go to Central Park on a rainy day?

A: Yes, you can still visit Central Park on a rainy day, but be prepared for wet and slippery conditions.

Q: Are Broadway shows canceled on rainy days?

A: No, Broadway shows are rarely canceled due to rain. However, it’s always a good idea to check with the theater before heading out.

Q: Can I still go shopping on a rainy day?

A: Yes, there are plenty of indoor shopping options throughout the city.

Don’t let the rain ruin your plans in New York City. With so many indoor activities to choose from, you can still have a great time on a rainy day. Whether you’re into museums, movies, or shopping, there’s something for everyone.
