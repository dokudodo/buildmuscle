Things To Do On Father s Day Melbourne 2023

If you're looking for the perfect way to celebrate Father's Day in Melbourne this year, you're in luck! There are plenty of fun activities and events to choose from that will make your dad feel special and appreciated. Read on for our top picks.

1. Visit the Melbourne Zoo

The Melbourne Zoo is always a great place to visit, and Father’s Day is no exception. You and your dad can spend the day exploring the exhibits and seeing all sorts of incredible animals. Make sure to check out the Zoo's Father's Day activities, which may include special talks and tours.

2. Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride

If your dad loves adventure and breathtaking views, consider taking him on a hot air balloon ride over Melbourne. You'll get a unique perspective of the city and create lasting memories together.

3. Enjoy a Round of Golf

For dads who love to hit the links, spending the day on a golf course can be a great way to bond. Check out some of Melbourne's top golf courses, such as Royal Melbourne Golf Club or Kingston Heath Golf Club, for a memorable Father's Day activity.

4. Go on a Brewery Tour

If your dad is a beer enthusiast, why not take him on a brewery tour? Melbourne has a thriving craft beer scene, and there are plenty of tours available that will take you to some of the city's best breweries.

5. Have a Picnic in the Park

If you're looking for a more low-key way to celebrate Father's Day, consider having a picnic in one of Melbourne's beautiful parks. Pack your dad's favorite foods and drinks and spend the day relaxing in the sunshine.

6. Take a Cooking Class

For dads who love to cook, taking a cooking class together can be a fun and educational way to spend Father's Day. Check out some of Melbourne's top cooking schools, such as The Essential Ingredient or The Cooking School at the Abbotsford Convent.

7. Attend a Sporting Event

For sports-loving dads, attending a game or match can be a thrilling way to spend Father's Day. Check out the schedule for Melbourne's top teams, such as the Melbourne Cricket Club or the Melbourne Victory Football Club, and plan accordingly.

8. Visit a Museum or Gallery

If your dad is a history buff or art lover, spending the day at a museum or gallery can be a great way to bond. Melbourne has plenty of options to choose from, such as the National Gallery of Victoria or the Melbourne Museum.

9. Take a Day Trip

If you have the time and resources, consider taking a day trip outside of Melbourne for Father's Day. You can explore nearby destinations such as the Great Ocean Road or the Yarra Valley, or even venture further afield to places like the Grampians or Phillip Island.

10. Cook Dad His Favorite Meal

Finally, one of the simplest and most heartfelt ways to celebrate Father's Day is to cook your dad his favorite meal. Whether it's a classic barbecue or a gourmet feast, the effort and thoughtfulness will be appreciated.

In Conclusion

With so many great options to choose from, there's no excuse not to make this Father's Day one to remember. Whether you opt for adventure, relaxation, or something in between, spending quality time with your dad is the most important thing. Happy Father's Day!
