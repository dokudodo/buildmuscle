Are you planning a trip to New York, but worried about the unpredictable weather? Don't let the rain dampen your spirits! There are plenty of indoor activities to enjoy in the city that never sleeps. Here are the best things to do in New York on a rainy day.

Visit Museums

With over 80 museums in New York City, you'll never run out of options for a rainy day activity. The Metropolitan Museum of Art and the American Museum of Natural History are popular choices, but don't forget about the lesser-known gems such as the Tenement Museum and the Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum.


Catch a Show in Theaters

New York is famous for its Broadway shows, but there are also plenty of off-Broadway and off-off-Broadway productions to choose from. Check out Broadway.com or Telecharge for tickets.


Go Shopping

Stay dry by shopping at one of New York's many indoor malls such as the Westfield World Trade Center or the Shops at Columbus Circle. You can also explore the iconic Macy's or the famous Bergdorf Goodman.


Play Games in Arcades

If you're looking for some old-school fun, head to one of New York's arcades such as Barclays Center Arcade or Modern Pinball NYC.


Relax in Spas

Take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city by treating yourself to a spa day. There are plenty of options to choose from such as the luxurious Mandarin Oriental Spa or the budget-friendly Ohm Spa & Lounge.


Indulge in Food and Drinks

New York is a foodie's paradise, and rainy days are the perfect excuse to indulge. Try the famous hot dogs or pizza, or explore the city's diverse cuisine scene. You can also visit one of the many rooftop bars such as the 230 Fifth or the Top of the Standard.


Experience Virtual Reality

Escape the rainy day by immersing yourself in a virtual world. Visit VR World NYC or IMMERSION NYC for a unique and exciting experience.


Visit the Aquarium

Explore the underwater world at the New York Aquarium. It's a great activity for families or anyone who loves marine life.


Explore the Observatory

Get a bird's eye view of New York City by visiting one of the many observatories such as the One World Observatory or the Empire State Building Observatory.


Try Escape Rooms

Challenge yourself by trying one of New York's many escape rooms such as Escape the Room NYC or Komnata Quest.


Tips for a Rainy Day in New York

  • Bring an umbrella or raincoat.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking on wet sidewalks.
  • Check the weather forecast before heading out.
  • Consider booking tickets for indoor activities in advance.

Pros and Cons of a Rainy Day in New York

While rain can put a damper on outdoor activities, it also presents an opportunity to explore the city's indoor offerings. Plus, you'll avoid crowds and potentially save money on outdoor attractions. However, getting around the city may be more challenging and the weather can be unpredictable.


Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear on a rainy day in New York? Wear comfortable shoes for walking on wet sidewalks and bring an umbrella or raincoat to stay dry.

What are the best indoor activities to do in New York on a rainy day? Visit museums, catch a show in theaters, go shopping, play games in arcades, relax in spas, indulge in food and drinks, experience virtual reality, visit the aquarium, explore the observatory, or try escape rooms.

How can I avoid crowds on a rainy day in New York? Consider booking tickets for indoor activities in advance to avoid waiting in long lines.


Don't let a little rain ruin your New York trip. Embrace the opportunity to discover the city's indoor offerings and have a memorable time.
