Welcome to our guide on push day! This workout day is focused on exercises that engage your chest, shoulders, and triceps. In this post, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on what you should do on push day, including exercises, tips, and FAQs. Let's get started!

What is Push Day?

Push day is a term used to describe a workout day that focuses on exercises that involve pushing away from your body. This includes chest, shoulder, and tricep exercises. The goal of push day is to build upper body strength, increase muscle mass, and improve overall fitness.

What Exercises Should You Do on Push Day?

There are many exercises you can do on push day, and it's important to choose exercises that work for your fitness level and goals. Here are some exercises to consider:

  • Bench press
  • Push-ups
  • Shoulder press
  • Lateral raises
  • Tricep dips
  • Tricep extensions

How to Do Push Day Exercises

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do some of the most common push day exercises:

Bench Press

1. Lie down on a flat bench with your feet flat on the ground and your back arched.

2. Grip the bar with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.

3. Lower the bar to your chest, then press it back up.

4. Repeat for your desired number of reps and sets.

Push-Ups

1. Start in a plank position, with your hands shoulder-width apart.

2. Lower your body down so that your chest touches the ground.

3. Push back up to the starting position.

4. Repeat for your desired number of reps and sets.

Shoulder Press

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your knees slightly bent.

2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.

3. Push the dumbbells up over your head, then lower them back down.

4. Repeat for your desired number of reps and sets.

Tips for Push Day

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your push day workout:

  • Warm up properly before starting your workout.
  • Choose weights that challenge you but are still manageable.
  • Focus on maintaining proper form throughout each exercise.
  • Take breaks between sets as needed.
  • Stay hydrated during your workout.

FAQs About Push Day

Here are some frequently asked questions about push day:

How Often Should You Do Push Day?

You can do push day once or twice a week, depending on your fitness goals and schedule.

Can You Do Push Day at Home?

Yes, you can do push day exercises at home with dumbbells, resistance bands, or bodyweight exercises like push-ups.

Do You Need to Lift Heavy Weights on Push Day?

No, you don't need to lift heavy weights to have an effective push day workout. Focus on proper form and choosing weights that challenge you without putting you at risk of injury.

Pros and Cons of Push Day

Like any workout routine, push day has its pros and cons. Here are some to consider:

Pros

  • Builds upper body strength and muscle mass.
  • Improves overall fitness and athletic performance.
  • Can be done with minimal equipment.

Cons

  • Can put strain on the shoulders and wrists if not done properly.
  • May not be suitable for everyone, especially those with existing injuries or conditions.
  • Should be balanced with other types of workouts for overall fitness.

That concludes our guide on what to do on push day! Remember to warm up properly, choose weights that challenge you, and maintain proper form throughout each exercise. Happy lifting!
