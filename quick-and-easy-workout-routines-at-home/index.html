How to Get Started

Before you begin any workout routine, it is important to consult with your doctor to ensure that you are healthy enough to exercise. Once you have the green light, you can get started with these quick and easy workout routines at home. All you need is a small space and some motivation!

Step-by-Step Guide to Quick and Easy Workout Routines

Here are some simple and effective workout routines that you can do at home:

1. Bodyweight Squats

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Lower your body down as if you are sitting in a chair, keeping your knees in line with your toes. Push back up to your starting position and repeat for 3 sets of 10 reps.
