What Should I Do Every Day In Animal Crossing

Animal Crossing is a popular life simulation game that has captured the hearts of many gamers worldwide. It's a game that allows you to live a virtual life in a peaceful village filled with friendly animal characters. There's no right or wrong way to play Animal Crossing, but to fully enjoy the game, you need to know what to do every day. In this article, we'll take a look at what you should do every day in Animal Crossing and how to make the most out of your gaming experience.

Daily Routine

The first thing you should do every day in Animal Crossing is to check your daily routine. This includes checking your mailbox for any letters from your villagers or the game's developers. You should also check the bulletin board for any events or announcements that may be happening in the village. Once you've checked your daily routine, you can start your day.

Farming

Farming is an essential part of Animal Crossing. You can plant trees, flowers, and vegetables in your village, which will help you earn bells (the game's currency). You can also sell your crops to Tom Nook, the game's shopkeeper, for a good price. Make sure to water your crops every day to ensure they grow healthy and strong.

Fishing

Fishing is another essential part of Animal Crossing. You can catch various types of fish in the rivers and oceans surrounding your village. Fishing is an excellent way to earn bells and complete your museum's fish collection. Make sure to check the weather and time of day to know which fish are available to catch.

Bug Catching

Similar to fishing, you can also catch various types of bugs in Animal Crossing. You can find bugs in the grass, trees, and flowers around your village. Catching bugs is an excellent way to earn bells and complete your museum's bug collection. Make sure to check the weather and time of day to know which bugs are available to catch.

Visiting Friends

Animal Crossing is also a social game. You can visit your friends' villages and interact with their villagers. You can also trade items and bells with your friends. Visiting your friends' villages is an excellent way to make new friends and learn new things about the game.

Decorating

Animal Crossing allows you to decorate your house and village however you like. You can purchase furniture, wallpaper, and flooring from Tom Nook's shop or other villagers. You can also customize your items to make them unique. Decorating your house and village is an excellent way to express your creativity and make your village stand out.

Shopping

Tom Nook's shop is the main shopping destination in Animal Crossing. You can purchase various items from the shop, including furniture, clothing, and tools. The shop's inventory changes every day, so make sure to check it often for new items. You can also purchase items from other villagers or the game's online store, Nook Shopping.

Checking Mail

As mentioned earlier, you should check your mailbox every day for any letters from your villagers or the game's developers. You can also receive gifts from your friends or the game's special events. Make sure to check your mailbox regularly to ensure you don't miss out on any exciting events or gifts.

Selling Items

You can sell items you don't need or want at Tom Nook's shop. Selling items is an excellent way to earn bells and make room for new items. Make sure to check the prices of the items before selling them to ensure you get the best price.

Upgrading Home

You can upgrade your house in Animal Crossing by paying off your mortgage to Tom Nook. Upgrading your house allows you to have more rooms and space to decorate. You can also upgrade your house's exterior to make it look unique. Make sure to save up your bells to upgrade your house and make it your dream home.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are various things you can do every day in Animal Crossing to make the most out of your gaming experience. From farming to fishing, bug catching to decorating, there's never a dull moment in Animal Crossing. Make sure to check your daily routine and enjoy all the game has to offer.
