Morrisons Morning Rolls Calories

A typical Morrisons morning roll contains around 160-180 calories. This may vary depending on the size and ingredients used. If you add butter or jam to your roll, the calorie count will increase significantly. It's important to keep in mind that these rolls are high in carbohydrates and may not be the best choice if you're trying to lose weight or follow a low-carb diet.

How to Make Morrisons Morning Rolls Healthier

If you're concerned about the high calorie count in Morrisons morning rolls, there are some simple ways to make them healthier. Here are a few tips:

  • Choose whole grain rolls instead of white rolls.
  • Avoid adding butter or jam to your roll.
  • Fill your roll with healthy ingredients such as avocado, tomato, and lean protein.

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Morrisons Morning Rolls

Do you want to make your own Morrisons morning rolls at home? Here's a simple recipe:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the dough and leave it to rise for an hour in a warm place.
  5. Punch down the dough and divide it into six pieces.
  6. Shape each piece into a roll and place them on a baking sheet.
  7. Cover the rolls and leave them to rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Bake the rolls in a preheated oven at 200°C for 15-20 minutes.
  9. Remove from the oven and cool on a wire rack.

Tips for Making the Perfect Morrisons Morning Rolls

Here are some tips to help you make the perfect Morrisons morning rolls:

  • Use warm water to activate the yeast.
  • Knead the dough for at least 10 minutes to develop gluten.
  • Let the dough rise in a warm place to ensure a light and fluffy texture.
  • Brush the rolls with egg wash before baking for a shiny crust.

FAQ about Morrisons Morning Rolls

Q. Are Morrisons morning rolls vegan?

A. Morrisons morning rolls are not vegan as they contain milk and/or egg. However, you can easily make a vegan version at home by using plant-based milk and egg replacer.

Q. How long do Morrisons morning rolls last?

A. Morrisons morning rolls are best consumed within a day or two. You can freeze them for up to a month, but they may lose some of their texture and flavor.

Q. Can I make Morrisons morning rolls in advance?

A. Yes, you can make Morrisons morning rolls in advance and store them in an airtight container. Reheat them in the oven for a few minutes before serving.

Pros and Cons of Eating Morrisons Morning Rolls

Here are some pros and cons of eating Morrisons morning rolls:

Pros:

  • Quick and easy breakfast option
  • Can be customized with various fillings
  • Inexpensive

Cons:

  • High in calories and carbohydrates
  • May not be suitable for those following a low-carb diet
  • May not be as nutritious as other breakfast options
