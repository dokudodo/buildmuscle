If you are a morning person, you will love London. The city is full of activities that will keep you entertained and energized throughout the day. In this blog post, we will share with you some of the best things to do early morning in London. From exploring the city's famous landmarks to enjoying a cup of coffee at a local café, there is something for everyone. So, let's get started!

Explore London's Famous Landmarks

London is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in the world, and the best time to explore them is early in the morning before the crowds arrive. Some of the must-see landmarks include the Tower Bridge, Big Ben, the London Eye, and Buckingham Palace. You can also take a walking tour to learn more about the history and culture of the city.

Exercise at Hyde Park or Regent's Park

If you are looking for a place to exercise and enjoy the outdoors, Hyde Park and Regent's Park are perfect options. Hyde Park is one of the largest parks in London, and it offers a variety of activities such as running, cycling, and horse riding. Regent's Park is known for its beautiful gardens and is home to the London Zoo.

Enjoy a Delicious Breakfast at a Local Café

London is famous for its breakfast options, and you can find a variety of delicious cafes and restaurants that serve traditional British breakfast. Some of the popular options include bacon and eggs, porridge, and smoked salmon. You can also try some international options such as French croissants or Turkish breakfast.

Visit a Local Market

London is home to many local markets that offer a unique shopping experience. Some of the popular markets include Borough Market, Camden Market, and Portobello Road Market. You can find a variety of goods such as fresh produce, handmade crafts, and vintage clothing.

Visit a Museum or Art Gallery

If you are interested in history and art, London has many museums and art galleries that are worth visiting. Some of the popular options include the British Museum, Tate Modern, and the National Gallery. Many of these museums and galleries offer early morning admission, so you can beat the crowds and enjoy the exhibits in peace.

Take a Boat Trip on the Thames

The River Thames is one of the most iconic features of London, and you can enjoy a boat trip to see the city from a different perspective. There are many boat tours that offer early morning departures, and you can enjoy breakfast on board while taking in the stunning views.

Relax in one of London's Beautiful Parks

London is home to many beautiful parks that are perfect for relaxing and enjoying the outdoors. Some of the popular options include St. James's Park, Kensington Gardens, and Greenwich Park. You can bring a book or a picnic and spend a few hours enjoying the peaceful surroundings.

Go Shopping on Oxford Street

Oxford Street is one of the most famous shopping destinations in London, and it offers a wide range of shops and boutiques. If you are an early bird, you can beat the crowds and enjoy a peaceful shopping experience before the stores get busy.

Visit a Historic Church

London is home to many historic churches that are worth visiting. Some of the popular options include St. Paul's Cathedral, Westminster Abbey, and Southwark Cathedral. You can enjoy the stunning architecture and learn about the history of the city.

Try Some Traditional British Food

London is known for its diverse food scene, and you can find a variety of international cuisines. However, if you want to experience traditional British food, there are many options to choose from. Some of the popular dishes include fish and chips, pie and mash, and roast beef with Yorkshire pudding.

Tips for Early Morning Activities in London

  • Check the weather forecast before heading out.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking and exploring.
  • Bring a water bottle and snacks for the day.
  • Plan your itinerary in advance to make the most of your time.
  • Take public transportation or walk to avoid traffic and delays.

FAQs

What time do things open in London?

Most attractions and shops in London open between 9am and 10am, but some may open earlier or later. It is best to check the opening hours in advance to avoid disappointment.

Are there any free things to do early morning in London?

Yes, there are many free things to do early morning in London such as visiting the parks, exploring the landmarks, and walking tours.

Pros and Cons of Early Morning Activities in London

Pros:

  • Avoiding crowds and long lines.
  • Enjoying peaceful and quiet surroundings.
  • Experiencing the city in a different light.
  • Having more time to explore and see more things.

Cons:

  • Waking up early can be a challenge.
  • Some attractions and shops may not be open yet.
  • Public transportation may be limited early in the morning.

Overall, early morning activities in London can be a great way to start your day and make the most of your time in the city. Whether you prefer exploring the landmarks, enjoying a delicious breakfast, or relaxing in one of the beautiful parks, there is something for everyone. So, set your alarm clock and get ready to experience the best of London in the morning!
