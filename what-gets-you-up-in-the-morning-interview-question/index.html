Description

The "what gets you up in the morning" interview question is a common one that potential employers ask in job interviews. It is a question that is designed to help the interviewer determine what motivates you and what drives you to succeed. This question is often asked as an icebreaker to help the interviewer get to know you better and to open up the conversation to more specific questions related to the job position.


How to Answer the Question

Answering the "what gets you up in the morning" interview question can be tricky. You want to provide an honest answer that showcases your motivation and drive without giving away too much personal information. The best way to answer this question is to be specific and give examples of what motivates you to succeed.


Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to answer the "what gets you up in the morning" interview question:

  1. Think about your personal and professional goals.
  2. Identify what motivates you to achieve those goals.
  3. Provide specific examples of what motivates you.
  4. Explain how your motivation drives you to succeed.

Tips for Answering the Question

Here are some tips for answering the "what gets you up in the morning" interview question:

  • Be honest and genuine in your answer.
  • Focus on your professional goals rather than personal ones.
  • Provide specific examples of what motivates you.
  • Explain how your motivation helps you achieve your goals.

Solution for a Great Answer

A great answer to the "what gets you up in the morning" interview question is one that showcases your motivation and drive. To provide a great answer, you should focus on your professional goals and provide specific examples of what motivates you to achieve those goals. You should also explain how your motivation helps you succeed in your career.


FAQs

Q: Should I mention personal goals in my answer?

A: While it is important to be honest in your answer, it is best to focus on your professional goals rather than personal ones.

Q: How specific should I be in my answer?

A: You should be as specific as possible in your answer. Provide examples of what motivates you and how that motivation helps you succeed in your career.

Q: Is it okay to mention money as a motivator?

A: While money is a common motivator, it is best to focus on other factors such as personal and professional growth.


Pros and Cons of the Question

Pros:

  • Helps the interviewer get to know you better.
  • Allows you to showcase your motivation and drive.
  • Opens up the conversation to more specific questions related to the job position.

Cons:

  • Can be a difficult question to answer.
  • May reveal personal information that you are not comfortable sharing.
  • May not be relevant to the job position.
