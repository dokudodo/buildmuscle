Oats Upma

Oats Upma is a healthy and quick breakfast recipe that is perfect for weight loss. It is made with oats, vegetables, and spices, making it a high-fiber and low-calorie meal. Here's how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, and chana dal. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions, green chili, and sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
  4. Add water and let it come to a boil.
  5. Then add oats and mix well. Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Vegetable Dalia

Dalia is a popular breakfast dish in India that is made from broken wheat. It is high in fiber and low in calories, making it an excellent option for weight loss. Here's how you can make Vegetable Dalia:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dalia
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 capsicum, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, and chana dal. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions, and sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
  4. Add vegetables and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add water and let it come to a boil.
  6. Then add dalia and mix well. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Moong Dal Chilla

Moong Dal Chilla is a popular breakfast recipe in India that is not only delicious but also healthy. It is made with moong dal, vegetables, and spices, making it a protein-rich and low-calorie meal. Here's how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup moong dal
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • Oil for cooking
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing

Method:

  1. Soak moong dal in water for 3-4 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind the dal to a smooth batter.
  3. Add onions, tomatoes, green chili, cumin seeds, and salt to the batter. Mix well.
  4. Add water as required to make a thin batter.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan and grease it with oil.
  6. Pour a ladleful of batter on the pan and spread it in a circular motion.
  7. Cook on medium flame until it turns golden brown on one side.
  8. Flip it and cook on the other side until it turns golden brown.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with chutney.

Egg White Omelette

Egg White Omelette is a high-protein and low-calorie breakfast recipe that is perfect for weight loss. It is made with egg whites, vegetables, and spices, making it a nutritious and delicious meal. Here's how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing

Method:

  1. Whisk egg whites in a bowl and add salt and black pepper powder. Mix well.
  2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan and add onions and green chili. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and sauté for a minute.
  4. Pour the egg mixture on the pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Cook on low flame until it sets.
  6. Flip it and cook on the other side for a minute.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Green Peas Paratha

Green Peas Paratha is a healthy and filling breakfast recipe that is perfect for weight loss. It is made with whole wheat flour, green peas, and spices, making it a high-fiber and low-calorie meal. Here's how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup boiled green peas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • Oil for cooking

Method:

  1. Mash the green peas and add onions, green chili, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Add whole wheat flour and mix well.
  3. Add water as required to make a soft dough.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a circle.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan and cook the paratha on both sides until it turns golden brown.
  6. Brush oil on both sides while cooking.
  7. Serve hot with curd or chutney.
