Are you a vegan looking to gain some weight? Contrary to popular belief, it is possible to put on weight on a plant-based diet. In fact, a well-planned vegan diet can provide all the necessary nutrients for weight gain. Here are some healthy, delicious and easy-to-make vegan recipes that can help you achieve your weight gain goals.

Avocado Toast

This is a quick and easy recipe that can be eaten for breakfast, lunch or as a snack. Avocado is a good source of healthy fats and can help you gain weight.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to:

  1. To make the avocado spread, mash the avocado in a bowl and add lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Toast the bread slices.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture on the toast and top with sliced tomatoes.
  4. Enjoy!

Chickpea Curry

Chickpeas are an excellent source of protein and can help you gain muscle mass. This curry is easy to make and can be served with rice or bread.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp. cumin powder
  • 1 tsp. coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp. turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp. garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp. oil
  • Water as needed

How to:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onion and minced garlic.
  2. Saute until the onion turns translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomato and cook until it becomes soft.
  4. Add all the spices and salt, and cook for a minute.
  5. Add the chickpeas and enough water to cover them.
  6. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes or until the chickpeas are soft and the curry has thickened.
  7. Serve with rice or bread.

Nutty Granola Bowl

This is a delicious and nutritious breakfast bowl that can be customized to your liking. It contains healthy fats, protein and complex carbohydrates.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup of chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans)
  • 1/4 cup of dried fruit (raisins, cranberries, apricots)
  • 1/4 cup of seeds (pumpkin, sunflower, chia)
  • 1 tbsp. of coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp. of maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp. of cinnamon powder
  • 1/2 tsp. of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of non-dairy milk

How to:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F (180Â°C).
  2. Mix the oats, nuts, dried fruit, seeds, coconut oil, maple syrup, cinnamon powder and vanilla extract in a bowl.
  3. Spread the mixture on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  4. Let it cool and transfer to a bowl.
  5. Pour non-dairy milk over the granola and enjoy!

Sweet Potato Fries

Sweet potatoes are an excellent source of complex carbohydrates and can help you gain weight. This recipe is a healthy alternative to regular fries.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp. of olive oil
  • 1 tsp. of paprika powder
  • 1 tsp. of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F (220Â°C).
  2. Peel and cut the sweet potatoes into thin fries.
  3. In a bowl, mix the olive oil, paprika powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  4. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly.
  5. Spread the fries on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until crispy.
  6. Enjoy!

Peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie

This smoothie is a delicious and easy way to consume healthy fats and protein. It can be consumed as a snack or a post-workout drink.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tbsp. of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of non-dairy milk
  • 1 tsp. of maple syrup (optional)

How to:

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you gain weight on a plant-based diet:

  • Consume enough calories to meet your daily needs. Use a calorie tracking app to help you determine your calorie needs.
  • Eat frequently throughout the day to increase your calorie intake.
  • Incorporate healthy fats into your diet such as avocados, nuts, seeds and olive oil.
  • Choose whole foods over processed foods.
  • Include protein-rich foods such as beans, lentils, tofu and tempeh in your diet.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking enough water and other fluids.

FAQ

Q: Is it possible to gain weight on a vegan diet?

A: Yes, it is possible to gain weight on a vegan diet. A well-planned vegan diet can provide all the necessary nutrients for weight gain.

Q: Can I consume vegan junk food to gain weight?

A: Consuming vegan junk food may help you gain weight, but it is not a healthy option. It is important to choose whole, nutrient-dense foods to support your overall health.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • A plant-based diet can provide all the necessary nutrients for weight gain.
  • Consuming a vegan diet can help lower the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Choosing whole, nutrient-dense foods can support overall health.

Cons:

  • It may be challenging to consume enough calories on a vegan diet.
  • It may require more planning and preparation to ensure adequate nutrient intake.

With these healthy vegan recipes and tips, gaining weight on a plant-based diet can be easy and delicious. Remember to consult with a healthcare professional before making any significant changes to your diet.
