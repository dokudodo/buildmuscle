If you are a male non-vegetarian who wants to gain weight, you need to plan your diet accordingly. Weight gain is a slow process and requires a proper diet plan and workout routine. In this article, we will discuss the diet chart for weight gain for male non-vegetarians.

How to Gain Weight?

Gaining weight is not easy, especially for those who have a high metabolism rate. To gain weight, you need to consume more calories than you burn. You need to increase your calorie intake by 500-1000 calories per day to gain 1-2 pounds per week.

Step-by-Step Diet Chart for Weight Gain for Male Non-Vegetarian

Here is a step-by-step diet chart for weight gain for male non-vegetarians:

1. Breakfast:

Start your day with a high-calorie breakfast. You can have 2-3 eggs, 2-3 slices of bacon, whole wheat toast, and a glass of full-fat milk.

2. Mid-Morning Snack:

You can have a protein shake or a handful of nuts for your mid-morning snack.

3. Lunch:

Your lunch should consist of a combination of protein, carbs, and healthy fats. You can have grilled chicken, brown rice, and a salad with avocado dressing.

4. Afternoon Snack:

You can have a fruit smoothie or a protein bar for your afternoon snack.

5. Dinner:

Your dinner should be high in protein and carbs. You can have grilled salmon, sweet potato, and steamed veggies.

6. Late-Night Snack:

You can have a bowl of Greek yogurt with honey and nuts for your late-night snack.

Tips for Weight Gain

Here are some tips for weight gain:

  • Consume more calories than you burn.
  • Eat protein-rich foods.
  • Eat carbs and healthy fats.
  • Drink plenty of water.
  • Get enough sleep.
  • Exercise regularly.

Solution for Weight Gain

The solution for weight gain is to consume more calories than you burn. You need to increase your calorie intake by 500-1000 calories per day to gain 1-2 pounds per week. You also need to eat protein-rich foods, carbs, and healthy fats. Exercise regularly and get enough sleep to support your weight gain goals.

FAQs

Q. Can I gain weight without eating non-vegetarian food?

A. Yes, you can gain weight without eating non-vegetarian food. You can consume high-calorie vegetarian foods such as nuts, seeds, beans, lentils, and whole grains.

Q. Can I gain weight without exercising?

A. No, you need to exercise to support your weight gain goals. Exercise helps you build muscle mass, which is essential for weight gain.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • You can gain weight and build muscle mass.
  • You can improve your overall health and fitness.

Cons:

  • You need to consume more calories than you burn, which can be challenging.
  • You need to exercise regularly to support your weight gain goals.

In conclusion, gaining weight requires a proper diet plan and workout routine. You need to consume more calories than you burn and eat protein-rich foods, carbs, and healthy fats. Exercise regularly and get enough sleep to support your weight gain goals. With a proper diet and workout routine, you can achieve your weight gain goals and improve your overall health and fitness.
