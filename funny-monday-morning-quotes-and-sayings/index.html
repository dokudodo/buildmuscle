Monday mornings can be tough, but injecting a little humor into the day can make all the difference. Here are some funny Monday morning quotes and sayings to start your week off on the right foot.

"I have a love-hate relationship with Mondays."

Many people can relate to this Monday morning quote. You might dread the start of the workweek, but at the same time, you know that Monday is a fresh start to a new week.

"Monday is a fresh start. It's never too late to dig in and start a new journey of success."

This quote is a great reminder that Monday doesn't have to be a drag. Instead, it's an opportunity to start fresh and set yourself up for a successful week ahead.

"I'm not a morning person, but if I woke up every morning to a room full of puppies, I might change my mind."

This funny Monday morning quote is sure to put a smile on your face. Who wouldn't want to wake up to a room full of puppies?

"Monday is a good day to have a great day."

This quote is a great mantra to live by. Instead of dreading Monday, embrace it as an opportunity to make the most of the day and have a great start to the week.

"I don't hate Mondays, I'm just not a big fan of them."

This is a funny way to express your feelings towards Monday. It's not that you hate it, you just don't love it either.

"Monday should be optional."

Many people would probably agree with this sentiment. Unfortunately, Monday isn't optional, so we might as well make the most of it.

"Monday mornings are the cruel jest of the gods."

This quote perfectly captures the feeling of dread that many people experience on Monday mornings. But remember, it's all about perspective!

"If Monday had a face, I would punch it."

This quote might be a little extreme, but it's a funny way to express your frustration towards Monday. Of course, we don't condone violence!

"I have a case of the Mondays."

This is a common phrase that people use to express their lack of enthusiasm towards Monday. But remember, it's all about mindset!

"Monday is the perfect day to correct last week's mistakes."

This quote is a great reminder that Monday is a fresh start. Instead of dwelling on last week's mistakes, use Monday as an opportunity to correct them and set yourself up for success.

Conclusion

Starting the week off with a little humor can make all the difference in your mindset. Whether you use these funny Monday morning quotes and sayings to make yourself laugh or to share with your coworkers, they're sure to put a smile on your face.
