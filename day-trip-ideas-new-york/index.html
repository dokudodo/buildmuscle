Day Trip Ideas New York A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Wednesday Jun 22, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a break from the hustle and bustle of New York City, day trips are a great way to explore the surrounding areas. From charming towns to scenic hikes, there are plenty of options for a memorable day trip. In this guide, we'll cover some of the best day trip ideas in New York.

Table of Contents

  • Scenic Hikes

Charming Towns

If you're looking for a quaint and charming escape from the city, there are plenty of towns within a few hours of New York that fit the bill. One such town is Hudson, NY. Located about two hours north of the city, Hudson boasts picturesque streets lined with historic buildings, eclectic shops, and a thriving arts scene. Other options include Beacon, NY, and Cold Spring, NY, both located along the Hudson River and offering stunning river views and plenty of outdoor activities.


Scenic Hikes

If you're an outdoor enthusiast, there are plenty of scenic hikes within a few hours of New York City. One popular option is Breakneck Ridge, located about an hour and a half north of the city. This challenging hike offers stunning views of the Hudson River and the surrounding mountains. Other options include the Appalachian Trail, which passes through New York and offers plenty of day hike options, and the Mohonk Preserve, located about two hours north of the city and offering over 8,000 acres of scenic trails.


Beaches

If you're looking for a day of sun, sand, and surf, there are plenty of beaches within a few hours of New York City. One of the most popular options is Jones Beach, located about an hour and a half east of the city. This beach offers miles of white sand, plenty of amenities, and even a boardwalk. Other options include Fire Island, located about two hours east of the city and accessible only by ferry, and Sandy Hook, located about an hour and a half south of the city in New Jersey.


Museums

If you're in the mood for some culture, there are plenty of museums within a few hours of New York City. One must-visit museum is the Storm King Art Center, located about an hour and a half north of the city. This outdoor sculpture park features over 100 sculptures by renowned artists and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Other options include the Dia:Beacon, located in Beacon, NY, and the Hudson River Museum, located in Yonkers, NY.


Wineries

If you're a wine lover, there are plenty of wineries within a few hours of New York City. One popular option is the Finger Lakes region, located about four hours northwest of the city. This region boasts over 100 wineries and offers stunning views of the surrounding lakes and countryside. Other options include the North Fork of Long Island, located about two hours east of the city, and the Hudson Valley Wine Trail, located about an hour and a half north of the city.


Conclusion

Whether you're looking for a charming town, a scenic hike, a day at the beach, some culture, or some wine, there are plenty of day trip options within a few hours of New York City. So pack a bag, hit the road, and explore all that the surrounding areas have to offer.
