Are you a fan of ABC's Sunday night lineup? If so, you'll want to mark your calendar for the 2022 season. This year promises to be full of exciting new shows and returning favorites. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the ABC Sunday night schedule for 2022, including what to expect, how to watch, and more.

New Shows

ABC has a number of new shows debuting on Sunday nights in 2022. One of the most highly anticipated is Queens, which follows a group of former hip-hop stars who reunite to reclaim their fame. Another new show is Maggie, a drama about a woman who becomes a surgeon after a personal tragedy. Other new shows include Triage, a medical drama, and The Wonder Years, a reboot of the classic 80s show.

Returning Favorites

In addition to the new shows, ABC has several returning favorites on its Sunday night schedule. One of the most popular is Grey's Anatomy, which is entering its 19th season. Other returning shows include The Rookie, A Million Little Things, and Supermarket Sweep.

How to Watch

If you want to watch ABC's Sunday night lineup, you have several options. The easiest way is to tune in to your local ABC affiliate at the scheduled time. You can also watch the shows on ABC's website or app, although you'll need a cable login to do so. Another option is to use a streaming service like Hulu Live or YouTube TV, which offer live TV streaming of ABC and other networks.

Tips for Watching

If you're planning to watch ABC on Sunday nights, here are a few tips to get the most out of your viewing experience:

  • Make sure you have a reliable internet connection or cable service
  • Set a reminder for when the show starts so you don't miss it
  • Prepare some snacks and drinks to enjoy while you watch
  • Consider watching with friends or family for a more social experience

Pros and Cons

As with any TV lineup, there are pros and cons to ABC's Sunday night schedule. Here are a few to consider:

Pros

  • Wide variety of shows to choose from
  • Several popular returning favorites
  • Easy to watch on TV or online

Cons

  • Some shows may not appeal to everyone
  • Ads can be annoying for some viewers
  • Not all shows are available online without a cable login

FAQ

Q: When does the ABC Sunday night schedule start?

A: The 2022 season of ABC's Sunday night lineup is expected to start in September.

Q: What time do the shows start?

A: The exact times may vary depending on your location and local affiliate, but most shows start at 8pm Eastern/7pm Central.

Q: Can I watch the shows online?

A: Yes, you can watch the shows on ABC's website or app, as well as on streaming services like Hulu Live or YouTube TV. However, you'll need a cable login to do so.

Q: Will there be any crossovers between the shows?

A: It's possible, but nothing has been confirmed yet. Keep an eye out for any announcements or teasers.

Q: What if I miss an episode?

A: You can usually watch the episode on ABC's website or app the next day, or on some streaming services. You can also set your DVR to record the show if you have cable.

Overall, ABC's Sunday night lineup for 2022 promises to be an exciting mix of new shows and returning favorites. Whether you watch on TV or online, be sure to tune in and enjoy the entertainment.
