Recipe 1: Paneer Paratha

Paneer Paratha is a popular Indian breakfast recipe that's high in protein and calories. It's made with whole wheat flour and stuffed with paneer (cottage cheese) and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 200g paneer, crumbled
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil

Directions:

  1. Mix whole wheat flour, salt, and water to make a soft dough.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix crumbled paneer, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  3. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll them out into circles.
  4. Place a spoonful of the paneer mixture in the center of each circle and fold the edges to seal the stuffing inside.
  5. Roll out the stuffed dough balls into parathas.
  6. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle and cook the parathas with oil until golden brown on both sides.

Recipe 2: Moong Dal Chilla

Moong Dal Chilla is a savory Indian pancake made with moong dal (mung beans) and spices. It's a great source of protein, fiber, and healthy carbs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup moong dal, soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil

Directions:

  1. Drain the soaked moong dal and grind it into a smooth paste.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, green chili, ginger, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt to the dal paste and mix well.
  3. Add water as needed to make a batter of pouring consistency.
  4. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle and grease it with oil.
  5. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the tawa and spread it into a thin circle.
  6. Cook on medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter.

Recipe 3: Poha

Poha is a popular Indian breakfast recipe made with flattened rice flakes, vegetables, and spices. It's a great source of carbs, fiber, and vitamins.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup poha (flattened rice flakes)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Handful of peanuts
  • Handful of fresh coriander leaves

Directions:

  1. Rinse the poha under running water and let it soak for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  3. When the seeds start to splutter, add chopped onion, tomato, and green chili.
  4. Add turmeric powder and salt and mix well.
  5. Add soaked poha and mix gently.
  6. Cover and cook for 5 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with peanuts and fresh coriander leaves.

Recipe 4: Oats Upma

Oats Upma is a healthy and filling breakfast recipe made with oats, vegetables, and spices. It's a great source of fiber, protein, and healthy fats.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Handful of peanuts
  • Handful of fresh coriander leaves

Directions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  2. When the seeds start to splutter, add chopped onion, tomato, and green chili.
  3. Add turmeric powder and salt and mix well.
  4. Add rolled oats and mix gently.
  5. Add water as needed to cook the oats.
  6. Cover and cook for 5 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with peanuts and fresh coriander leaves.

Recipe 5: Banana and Peanut Butter Smoothie

A smoothie is a quick and easy breakfast option that's easy to make and packed with nutrients. This smoothie recipe is high in calories, protein, and healthy fats.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp honey

Directions:

  1. Blend all the ingredients in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve.

Tips for Healthy Weight Gain

Here are some tips to help you gain weight in a healthy way:

  • Eat more calories than you burn.
  • Eat frequent meals throughout the day.
  • Focus on nutrient-dense foods like whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats.
  • Include strength training exercises in your workout routine to build muscle mass.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and other fluids.

FAQs

Q: Can I eat these breakfast recipes every day?

A: Yes, these breakfast recipes are healthy and nutritious and can be eaten every day as part of a balanced diet.

Q: Can I skip breakfast to lose weight?

A: No, skipping breakfast can actually make it harder for you to lose weight. Eating a healthy breakfast helps boost your metabolism and provides you with energy to start your day.

Q: Can I customize these recipes to my taste?

A: Yes, these recipes can be customized to your taste by adding or subtracting ingredients and adjusting the spices to your liking.

Try out these delicious Indian breakfast recipes for healthy weight gain and start your day off on the right foot!
