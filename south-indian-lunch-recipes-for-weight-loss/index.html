South Indian Lunch Recipes For Weight Loss

If you're someone who loves south Indian food and is also looking to lose weight, then you're in luck! There are plenty of delicious and healthy south Indian lunch recipes that can help you shed those extra pounds. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best south Indian lunch recipes for weight loss.

Sambar

Sambar is a lentil-based vegetable stew that is a staple in many south Indian households. It's packed with protein and fiber, making it a great option for weight loss. To make sambar, you'll need to boil lentils and vegetables together with a spice mix that typically includes coriander, cumin, turmeric, and chili powder. Serve it with a side of brown or white rice for a filling and satisfying meal.

Rasam

Rasam is a spicy and tangy soup that is made with tamarind, tomato, and a variety of spices. It's low in calories and high in flavor, making it a great option for weight loss. To make rasam, you'll need to boil tamarind and tomato together with a spice mix that typically includes cumin, coriander, and black pepper. Serve it hot with a side of steamed vegetables or a small portion of rice.

Poriyal

Poriyal is a dry vegetable dish that is typically made with shredded or chopped vegetables like cabbage, carrot, or beans. It's low in calories and high in fiber, making it a great option for weight loss. To make poriyal, you'll need to sauté the vegetables in a small amount of oil with mustard seeds and curry leaves. Serve it as a side dish with sambar or rasam.

Avial

Avial is a mixed vegetable dish that is cooked in coconut and yogurt gravy. It's low in calories and high in nutrients, making it a great option for weight loss. To make avial, you'll need to boil mixed vegetables like carrot, beans, and potato with a spice mix that typically includes cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Then, add coconut and yogurt to the mix and let it simmer for a few minutes. Serve it with a side of rice or chapati.

Thoran

Thoran is a dry vegetable dish that is typically made with grated coconut and chopped vegetables like cabbage, carrot, or beans. It's low in calories and high in fiber, making it a great option for weight loss. To make thoran, you'll need to sauté the vegetables in a small amount of oil with mustard seeds and curry leaves. Then, add grated coconut and let it cook for a few minutes. Serve it as a side dish with sambar or rasam.

Vegetable Curry

Vegetable curry is a spicy and flavorful dish that is made with a variety of vegetables like potato, carrot, and cauliflower. It's low in calories and high in nutrients, making it a great option for weight loss. To make vegetable curry, you'll need to sauté the vegetables in a small amount of oil with onion, garlic, and ginger. Then, add a spice mix that typically includes cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Let it simmer for a few minutes and serve it with a side of rice or chapati.

Dal

Dal is a lentil-based soup that is a staple in many south Indian households. It's packed with protein and fiber, making it a great option for weight loss. To make dal, you'll need to boil lentils with a spice mix that typically includes cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Serve it with a side of brown or white rice for a filling and satisfying meal.

Raita

Raita is a yogurt-based side dish that is typically served with rice or chapati. It's low in calories and high in protein, making it a great option for weight loss. To make raita, you'll need to mix yogurt with grated cucumber, carrot, and tomato. Add a spice mix that typically includes cumin, coriander, and salt to taste. Serve it cold as a side dish with any of the main dishes listed above.

Chapati

Chapati is a flatbread that is made with whole wheat flour. It's low in calories and high in fiber, making it a great option for weight loss. To make chapati, you'll need to mix whole wheat flour with water and knead it into a dough. Then, roll it out into thin circles and cook it on a hot griddle. Serve it hot with any of the main dishes listed above.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are plenty of delicious and healthy south Indian lunch recipes that can help you lose weight. From lentil-based soups and stews to dry vegetable dishes and spicy curries, there's something for everyone. So, the next time you're looking for a healthy and satisfying lunch option, give one of these recipes a try!
