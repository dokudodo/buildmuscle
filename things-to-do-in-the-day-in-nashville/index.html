If you're looking for a city that's full of culture, music, and history, then Nashville is the perfect place for you. Known as the Music City, Nashville has a lot to offer in terms of entertainment, food, and attractions. In this guide, we'll take you through some of the best things to do in the day in Nashville in the year 2023. From exploring its iconic landmarks to enjoying its vibrant music scene, Nashville has something for everyone.

Table of Contents

  • Exploring Landmarks
  • Enjoying the Music Scene
  • Indulging in Nashville's Food Scene
  • Discovering Nashville's Art Scene

Exploring Landmarks

Nashville is home to many iconic landmarks that are worth exploring. One of the most popular landmarks is the Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum. This museum is dedicated to preserving the history and culture of country music, and it features exhibits, artifacts, and interactive experiences that showcase the genre's evolution over time.

Another great landmark to visit is the Ryman Auditorium. This venue is a historic music hall that has been around since the late 1800s. Many famous musicians have performed here, and it's a must-visit spot for any music lover.

If you're interested in history, then the Belle Meade Plantation is a great place to visit. This plantation was once a grand estate that produced thoroughbred horses and fine wines. Today, it's a museum that offers guided tours and educational programs that explore the history of the plantation and its impact on Nashville.

Enjoying the Music Scene

As the Music City, Nashville is known for its vibrant music scene. One of the best ways to experience this is by visiting the honky-tonks on Broadway. These bars feature live music performances by local musicians, and they're a great place to get a taste of Nashville's music culture.

If you're looking for something more formal, then the Schermerhorn Symphony Center is a great place to catch a concert. This venue is home to the Nashville Symphony, and it hosts a variety of concerts throughout the year.

Another great way to experience Nashville's music scene is by visiting the Bluebird Cafe. This venue is a small, intimate space that's known for its songwriter nights, where up-and-coming musicians perform their original songs.

Indulging in Nashville's Food Scene

Nashville has a thriving food scene that's known for its Southern comfort food and hot chicken. One of the best places to try hot chicken is at Hattie B's. This restaurant serves up some of the spiciest chicken in town, and it's a must-visit spot for any foodie.

If you're looking for something more upscale, then the Husk restaurant is a great place to try. This restaurant features locally sourced ingredients and Southern-inspired dishes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Another great place to try is the Loveless Cafe. This restaurant is known for its biscuits, which are made from scratch every day. They're so popular that people come from all over just to try them.

Discovering Nashville's Art Scene

Nashville has a growing art scene that's worth exploring. One of the best places to start is the Frist Art Museum. This museum features a variety of art exhibits and interactive experiences that showcase the work of local and international artists.

Another great place to visit is the Cheekwood Estate and Gardens. This estate features a museum of art and botanical gardens that are perfect for a relaxing afternoon stroll.

If you're looking for something more offbeat, then the Hatch Show Print is a great place to visit. This print shop has been around since the late 1800s, and it's known for its iconic concert posters and hand-printed designs.

Exploring Nashville's Parks and Outdoor Spaces

Nashville has a variety of parks and outdoor spaces that are perfect for a day out. One of the most popular parks is the Centennial Park. This park features a replica of the Parthenon, as well as walking trails, picnic areas, and a lake.

If you're looking for something more adventurous, then the Radnor Lake State Park is a great place to visit. This park features hiking trails, wildlife, and scenic views of the lake.

Another great outdoor space to explore is the Cumberland River Greenway. This greenway runs along the Cumberland River and features walking and biking trails, as well as access to some of Nashville's iconic landmarks.

Conclusion

As you can see, Nashville has a lot to offer in terms of entertainment, food, and attractions. Whether you're interested in music, history, art, or the outdoors, there's something for everyone in this vibrant city. So, the next time you're in Nashville, be sure to check out some of these top things to do in the day in Nashville in 2023.
